I have strong skills in design pertaining to the beauty field.
I hold manicurist and gel nail certifications at the highest level of JNA (the Japan Nailist Association), and a beautician's license in hairdressing. Following my time as a hairdresser, I worked as a manicurist and have been involved in the beauty industry for many years, as such I am very well versed in nail and beauty salons.

I have lived in Australia for 10 years now.
I spent time in both New Zealand and Australia as part of working holiday program and then studied English at school in Canada.
I was tired of city life in Tokyo, Japan and moved to Tasmania, a place that is surrounded by beautiful nature.
I enjoy walking along the beach with my dog, going camping in the summer, having nice coffee at cafe and spending time relaxing in rich nature.

I have loved reading books since I was a child, and when I was in primary school, the number of books I could borrow from school on weekends was not enough, so I would borrow books from the public library as well and immerse myself in reading. To this day libraries and bookstores are a couple of my favourite places. Time flies when I walk around to browsing for a book. I feel like I am being drawn into the world of the story within the books; the thrill of turning to the next page...
For me, web design is the same. I get so much enjoyment from it, that I sometimes lose track of time and get lost in it.
I am committed to creating designs that combine my book-borne imagination and my various artistic and technical backgrounds, to bring your vision to life. Great web design allows the viewer to feel the world on the page and the wonder of what is beyond.
